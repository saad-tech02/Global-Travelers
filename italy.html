About This Destination

Italy is a country in Southern Europe, celebrated for its rich history, art, cuisine, and breathtaking landscapes. Known as the birthplace of the Roman Empire and the Renaissance, Italy offers travelers a journey through centuries of culture and innovation. Visitors can explore Rome, with its ancient Colosseum and Vatican City; Florence, the cradle of Renaissance art and architecture; and Venice, a romantic city built on canals. Beyond its cities, Italy is famous for the rolling hills of Tuscany, the dramatic Amalfi Coast, and the alpine beauty of the Dolomites.
